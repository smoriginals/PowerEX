About Powerex Multi Trading

Founded in 2021 by visionary entrepreneurs passionate about financial empowerment, Powerex Multi Trading bridges the gap between complex financial markets and everyday wealth-building.

2021 — Founded

A team of visionary entrepreneurs launched Powerex Multi Trading with one goal: to democratize premium financial investment opportunities for everyday people.

2022 — Rapid Growth

Expanded services to cover both Commodity Markets and Forex Trading, attracting thousands of members across the region within the first year.

2023–2024 — Global Reach

Introduced multi-tier income programs, launched the Binary and Level Income systems, and began awarding luxury rewards to top performers worldwide.

Today — 1 Million+ Members

Powerex continues to grow as one of the country's most trusted financial platforms, empowering members to achieve life-changing financial goals.
