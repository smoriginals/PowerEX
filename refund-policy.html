Refund Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Due to the nature of financial investments, all investments made on PowerX are subject to the following refund policy.

General Policy

Investments are non-refundable once a plan cycle has begun. We encourage all members to carefully review terms before investing.

Cancellation Window

You may cancel a newly created investment within 24 hours of the initial transaction, provided the plan has not yet been activated. Contact support immediately for cancellation requests.

Technical Errors

If a duplicate charge or technical error occurs, we will process a full refund within 5–7 business days. Please submit evidence via the Contact page.

Non-Refundable Cases

How to Request

Submit a refund request via Contact Us with your Order ID and reason. Our team will review within 3 business days.
